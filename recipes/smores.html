S'Mores

A tasty treat

Ingredients

  • graham crackers
  • chocolate
  • marshmallow

Steps

  1. Break the graham cracker in half, so you have two square pieces
  2. Break off 3 pieces of chocolate (leave the pieces together, so you have one continuous chunk)
  3. Roast the marshmallow to desired doneness
  4. Place the chocolate on one graham cracker piece
  5. Remove the marshmallow, and place it on top of the chocolate
  6. Top with the other graham cracker piece
